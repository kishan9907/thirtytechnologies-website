THE SURFACE BELOW IS WHERE WE WORK
01 — The firm

Decision quality over price prediction.

Every position follows a predefined rule set — researched, validated, and risk-gated before it ever touches capital. We trade our own book: no outside investors, no advisory, no signals for sale. This site is a front office, not a pitch.

Our first objective is survival. Returns follow consistency.

02 — Strategy universe

Four horizons, one regime-aware brain.

A shared AI-driven regime layer classifies conditions as trending, mean-reverting or volatile, and every strategy listens. The portfolio diversifies across time itself:

Hours

Intraday

Directional & volatility strategies on index derivatives. Flat by close.

Days

Weekly

Index options strategies built around the weekly expiry cycle.

Weeks

Monthly

Options selling and swing positions in liquid names.

Years

Multi-year

Systematic trend participation. Patient by design.

03 — From research to live

Most strategies never make it. That's the point.

01

Research

Hypotheses grounded in market structure, not curve-fit patterns.

02

Backtesting

Walk-forward analysis and Monte Carlo robustness, always net of costs.

03

Forward testing

Dummy orders against live markets — fills must happen at signal time, or the backtest was fiction.

04

Hand-held live

Minimal capital, maximal supervision, line-by-line parity with the backtest.

05

Autonomous

Gradual scale-up under continuous monitoring and hard, machine-enforced limits.

Capital earns the right to be deployed.

04 — Systems

Built in-house, end to end.

Real-time websocket market data flowing into a time-series datastore. A strategy engine, order management and scheduling that take a signal to a routed order in roughly one second — no human in the loop. Multi-broker connectivity for redundancy, containerised cloud infrastructure, and a monitoring layer watching heartbeats, data freshness and end-of-day reconciliation.

Machine learning where it earns its keep; production engineering everywhere.

05 — What we don't do

The constraints are the strategy.

Discretionary trades

If it isn't in the rule set, it doesn't happen.

Prediction bets

We size for distributions of outcomes, not forecasts.

Uncapped downside

Capital protection is non-negotiable, enforced in software.

Outside capital

No clients, no funds, no signals. Our own book only.
